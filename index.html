About Me

I'm Ryan Somerfield. I have a Master Degree in Systems Engineering and I have a passion for automation. I love Systems Engineering because it is at the intersection of Hardware and Software. I have over 2 years of experience developing hardware and software. I am currently working as an Applications Engineer at Precise Automation, a collaborative robotics manufacturer, where I develop hardware and software solutions, troubleshoot customer problems, and play with some of the coolest robots being built today.

I am originally from the suburbs of Chicago, Illinois. I attended the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign for both undergraduate and graduate school in Systems Engineering. After grad school, I moved out to the Bay Area to be among the best and brightest minds working in cutting edge industries. Having a background in Systems Engineering, I am comfortable working with both hardware and software, but I have a slight preference to software.

Interests

Technical Interests

I have a strong desire to constantly learn and grow, and I'm constantly exploring new areas of interest. It is likely that I've learned something new since updating this website, but here is a list of some of my past interests!

  • Machine Learning - Machine Learning is such a fun and dynamic field, and recently I have been especially interested in Reinforcement Learning and Deep Learning. I am developing a reinforcement learning environment to experiment with Deep Q Learning before tackling other variations of machine learning.
  • Autonomous Vehicles - I have been interested in Autonomous Vehicles for quite some time. As a Masters Student, I programmed an Autonomous Vehicle Lane Detection and Control Algorithm for a miniature differential drive vehicle.
  • Robotics - In a similar way to Autonomous Vehicles, I have been interested in Robotics since I was a kid. At University of Illinois, I worked in a lab developing control, path planning algorithms, and working with various different sensors related to robotics.
  • Automation - In general, my interests are focused around the task of automating. Whether its creating an efficient API to help program a system, developing an embedded systems application to solve a problem, or just writing software for the fun of it, I tend to put myself through a great deal of work in the name of automation!
